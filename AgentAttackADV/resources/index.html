Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island renowned for its forested volcanic mountains, typical rice paddies, and coral reef. Sacred sites such as the Uluwatu Temple, which stands atop a cliff, are found here. To the south, the seaside town of Kuta offers lively bars, while Seminyak, Sanur and Nusa Dua are popular locations. The island is also known for its yoga and meditation centers.

London

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is an extremely contemporary city with a history dating back to ancient Rome. At its centre stand the imposing Parliament House, the iconic clock tower known as Big Ben and Westminster Abbey, where the coronations of British monarchs take place. Across the Thames, the London Eye Ferris wheel offers spectacular views of the South Bank Cultural District and the entire city.

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most important cities in Europe, a world center of art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Urban architecture dating back to the 19th century is characterised by wide boulevards and the Seine. In addition to monuments such as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century Gothic Cathedral of Notre-Dame, the city is renowned for its traditional cafes and haute couture shops along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.
